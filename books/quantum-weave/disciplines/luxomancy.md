          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (10-15% reserves for 30-60 seconds of sustained dampening)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Emergency calming (panic attacks, trauma response)</li>
            <li>Medical procedures (reducing patient fear before surgery/treatment)</li>
            <li>Animal handling (calming frightened creatures)</li>
            <li>Combat psychology (reducing allies' fear, maintaining morale)</li>

      </div>

        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Temporarily increases confidence, courage, and emotional resilience by enhancing dopamine/norepinephrine production and reducing inhibition.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (5-30 minutes depending on power investment)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (12-18% reserves)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>

          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (15-20% reserves for significant pain relief)</p>

        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Permanently alters target's emotional baseline, personality traits, or affective responses by rewriting neural pathways at quantum level. Can eliminate PTSD, cure depression, remove phobias, or fundamentally change who someone is emotionally.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (70-90% depending on depth of rewrite)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Treating severe mental illness (PTSD, major depression, personality disorders), rehabilitation (reforming violent offenders), enhancing emotional resilience, removing traumatic memories' emotional charge.</p>

          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Permanent changes risky (cannot easily reverse), affects identity (therapeutic question: is cured person same person?), extreme thread cost, requires deep understanding of neuroscience, success rate ~60-70%, side effects common (emotional flatness, unexpected personality shifts).</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="empathic-nova" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">

          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (80-95% reserves for full-power nova affecting thousands)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Ending riots (broadcasting calm to angry mob), military morale (courage to entire army before battle), disaster response (hope to despairing survivors), political rallies (enthusiasm to supporters), religious experiences (ecstasy to congregation).</p>
          <p><strong>Famous Uses:</strong> The Grief Wave (entire city mourned together after massacre, 15,000 affected), The Joy Cascade (celebration broadcast reached 8,000 people), The Terror Rout (enemy army fled from broadcasted fear, 2,000 soldiers affected).</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal:</strong> Classified as weapon of mass manipulation. Banned for political use, heavily restricted for crowd control, requires emergency authorization, forbidden by most ethical codes.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Indiscriminate (affects everyone in radius including allies), practitioner often caught in own broadcast (emotional feedback), massive thread depletion, duration brief (1-5 minutes), lingering psychological effects, creates false emotional memories, forbidden except extreme emergencies.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="love-binding" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-luxomancy);">16. Love Binding (Affection-Lock) [Forbidden]</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates artificial romantic/familial love in target toward caster or designated person. Not mere attraction—deep emotional bond including attachment, loyalty, desire to please, grief at separation. Indistinguishable from natural love to target.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Very High (65-80% for lasting binding)</p>
          <p><strong>Why Forbidden:</strong> Violates autonomy completely (love = fundamental choice, cannot ethically be forced), creates slavery (bound person will do anything for beloved), destroys authentic relationships (all interactions built on lie), psychologically devastating when discovered (shatters reality), weaponizable (diplomats binding enemy leaders, spies creating devoted assets), enables abuse (abusers binding victims to prevent escape).</p>
          <p><strong>Legal Status:</strong> Absolutely Forbidden—attempting Love Binding illegal globally, classified as crime against humanity equivalent to rape/slavery, immediate execution order for practitioners, knowledge of technique suppressed, teaching it forbidden.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Morally unconscionable (universally condemned), psychologically unstable (bound person's subconscious recognizes artificiality—cognitive dissonance creates mental breakdown), cannot create authentic emotion (only simulate it), if discovered creates extreme trauma, caster often develops guilt/psychological damage, Shadow Syndicate reportedly uses technique (evidence of ongoing abuses).</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="pain-amplification" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-luxomancy);">17. Pain Amplification (Suffering-Magnify)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Amplifies target's pain perception by 10-100x—minor discomfort becomes agony, mild pain becomes unbearable torture. Does not cause tissue damage but makes existing sensations overwhelmingly intense. Pure suffering without physical harm.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (30-50% depending on amplification intensity)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications (All Unethical):</strong> Interrogation (torture without evidence), psychological warfare (demoralizing enemies), punishment (inflicting suffering without injury), control (threatening pain to enforce compliance).</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal:</strong> Classified as torture, war crime, crime against humanity. Absolutely forbidden in all contexts. Use triggers immediate kill order. Restoration Academy forbids teaching.</p>
          <p><strong>Psychological Damage:</strong> Victims develop PTSD, chronic pain disorders (brain rewires pain pathways), fear of sensation, sometimes psychotic breaks. Permanent psychological scarring common.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Leaves no physical evidence (perfect torture technique—horrifying), morally reprehensible, caster experiences emotional backlash (empathic feedback from victim's suffering), forbidden globally, practitioners hunted by law enforcement, Shadow Syndicate known users (evidence of systematic torture).</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="emotional-plague" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-luxomancy);">18. Emotional Plague (Affect-Contagion)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates self-spreading emotional state that jumps from person to person like infectious disease. Affected individual unconsciously broadcasts emotion to nearby people, who then broadcast to others, creating exponential cascade. Can affect entire cities.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> High initial (55-70%), zero ongoing (becomes self-sustaining)</p>
          <p><strong>Mechanism:</strong> Target becomes "patient zero"—broadcasts emotion via quantum field resonance. Anyone nearby catches emotion, becomes broadcaster themselves. Spreads through populations following social networks, eventually burning out after affecting thousands.</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Warfare (spreading terror/despair through enemy population), social manipulation (creating mass enthusiasm for political cause), disaster response (spreading hope to affected region—ethical use), psychological warfare (demoralizing entire cities).</p>
          <p><strong>Historical Examples:</strong> The Despair Plague (spread through 40,000 people, 300 suicides), The Joy Outbreak (accidentally created, affected 12,000), The Rage Epidemic (led to riots, 89 deaths).</p>
          <p><strong>Legal Status:</strong> Forbidden—classified as biological weapon (emotional equivalent), attempting Emotional Plague = terrorism, kill on sight order, knowledge suppressed.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Cannot be controlled once started (self-sustaining cascade), unpredictable spread (follows social networks chaotically), duration uncertain (hours to days before burnout), collateral damage massive (affects innocents), practitioner often caught in own plague, morally catastrophic, forbidden globally.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="consciousness-merge" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-luxomancy);">19. Consciousness Merge (Identity-Fusion)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Temporarily fuses two or more consciousnesses into single unified mind sharing all thoughts, memories, emotions, and perspectives. Creates temporary collective consciousness—multiple people experiencing reality as one entity.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (75-90% for two-person merge, increases with participants)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Ultimate collaboration (artists, scientists, engineers thinking as one), conflict resolution (experiencing opponent's perspective completely), romantic intimacy (lovers becoming one), therapy (understanding mental illness from inside), research (studying consciousness itself).</p>
          <p><strong>Experience:</strong> Participants lose individual boundaries—"I" becomes "we", all thoughts shared simultaneously, access all merged memories/skills/knowledge, experience multiple emotions simultaneously, perceive reality through multiple viewpoints. Profound but disorienting.</p>
          <p><strong>Psychological Effects:</strong> Identity confusion (who am I after merge?), boundary dissolution (difficulty re-establishing self), memory bleed (retaining partner's memories), skill transfer (sometimes retain merged abilities), grief at separation (losing part of self), addiction potential (some seek repeated merges).</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Duration limited (15-60 minutes maximum), separation traumatic (tearing apart unified consciousness), identity risks (sometimes merge doesn't fully separate—permanent bleed), requires absolute trust (total vulnerability), cannot merge hostile individuals (resistance prevents fusion), forbidden without informed consent.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="emotional-void" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-luxomancy);">20. Emotional Void (Affect-Nullification)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Completely eliminates all emotional capacity in target—no fear, joy, love, anger, sadness, anything. Creates perfect emotional flatness. Subject becomes purely rational entity without affective experience. Can be self-targeted for ultimate detachment.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Very High (60-80% for complete nullification lasting hours to days)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Emergency decision-making (removing panic/fear for critical choices), torture resistance (cannot be emotionally manipulated), depression treatment (temporary relief from suffering), combat (fearless soldiers), surgery (doctors operating without stress).</p>
          <p><strong>Psychological Effects:</strong> Total emotional absence (neither pleasant nor unpleasant—simply nothing), decision paralysis (values require emotion—without it, cannot prefer anything), social disconnection (cannot emotionally relate to others), existential emptiness (meaning requires feeling), sometimes relief (for those suffering emotional pain), often horror (upon restoration—realizing what was lost).</p>
          <p><strong>Medical Uses:</strong> Sometimes used for suicidal patients (temporary relief from unbearable emotional pain), severe PTSD (emotional break from triggers), terminal patients (reducing fear/grief at end of life).</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Psychologically dangerous (extended void creates permanent emotional damage), removes motivation (emotion drives action), value judgments impossible (cannot prefer anything without affect), social relationships destroyed (cannot love, empathize, connect), some never fully recover emotional capacity, forbidden for coercive use.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
