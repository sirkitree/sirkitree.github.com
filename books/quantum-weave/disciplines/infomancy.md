    <section id="novice-spells" style="max-width: 900px; margin: 4rem 0;">
      <h2 class="qw-title" style="margin-bottom: 2rem; color: var(--qw-infomancy);">Foundational Spells (Novice Tier)</h2>

      <div id="probe" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">1. Probe (Sense-Sweep)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Detects electromagnetic signatures and photon patterns within a 10-meter radius, revealing sources of information (heat signatures, electronic devices, recent light-based communication, residual EM fields).</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (sustained concentration, typically 30-60 seconds)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Low (5-10% of reserves for standard sweep)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Security sweeps (detecting hidden electronics, surveillance devices)</li>
            <li>Tracking (finding heat signatures of people/animals)</li>
            <li>Reconnaissance (mapping EM-active infrastructure)</li>
            <li>Diagnostics (sensing malfunction in electronic systems)</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="whisper" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">2. Whisper (Photon-Thread)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Sends a short message (up to ~50 words) to a specific recipient within 1 kilometer by modulating a targeted photon stream.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Instantaneous transmission, message delivered within 1-2 seconds</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Low-Moderate (10-15% reserves, scales with distance and message length)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Covert communication (silent, invisible messaging)</li>
            <li>Tactical coordination (squad communication during operations)</li>
            <li>Emergency alerts (quick warnings to allies)</li>
            <li>Social etiquette (discreet messages in formal settings)</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="record" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">3. Record (Pattern-Lock)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Captures and stores up to 60 seconds of sensory data (visual, auditory, or both) as encoded quantum states in ambient Weave structures.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Semi-persistent (data remains stable for 24-48 hours unless actively erased)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (15-20% reserves, scales with data complexity and duration)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Evidence preservation (recording crime scenes, contract negotiations)</li>
            <li>Personal memory backup (capturing important moments)</li>
            <li>Message drops (leaving information for later retrieval)</li>
            <li>Training (recording demonstrations for study)</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="query" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">4. Query (Logic-Probe)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Asks a binary (yes/no) question about locally available information and receives an answer based on electromagnetic resonance patterns.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Instantaneous (question posed, answer received within 2-5 seconds)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Low (5-8% reserves per query)</p>
          <p><strong>Limitations:</strong> Cannot read thoughts, predict future, or answer questions about information not physically encoded in environment.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="cipher" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">5. Cipher (Quantum-Scramble)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Encrypts a message or data packet using quantum key distribution before transmission. Only a recipient with the paired quantum key can decode it.</p>
          <p><strong>Security Level:</strong> Quantum-grade (eavesdropping attempt collapses quantum state, alerting sender/receiver)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (12-18% reserves, scales with data size)</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="beacon" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">6. Beacon (Persistent-Marker)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates a long-lasting electromagnetic signature at a specific location that can be detected by Infomancers using Probe or similar spells.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (1-7 days) or <strong>Permanent</strong> (indefinite until actively dispelled)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Temporary: Moderate (10-15% reserves)</li>
            <li>Permanent: High (25-35% reserves + embedded threads remain bound indefinitely)</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="echo" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">7. Echo (Temporal-Trace)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Reads residual electromagnetic and photon patterns from a location to reconstruct recent events (past 1-12 hours). Reveals visual "echoes" of activity.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (playback lasts as long as concentration maintained, typically 1-3 minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate-High (20-30% reserves depending on playback duration and data age)</p>
          <p><strong>Limitations:</strong> Cannot read thoughts or intangibles. Only captures events that left EM/photon traces. Quality degrades rapidly with time.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
    </section>

    <section id="intermediate-spells" style="max-width: 900px; margin: 4rem 0;">
      <h2 class="qw-title" style="margin-bottom: 2rem; color: var(--qw-infomancy);">Intermediate Spells (Practitioner Tier)</h2>

      <div id="network" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">8. Network (Multi-Link)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Establishes a shared communication network connecting 3-8 individuals simultaneously. All participants can send and receive thoughts, images, or sensory data.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (sustained concentration, typically 5-20 minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Range:</strong> Up to 500 meters radius from caster (hub)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> High-Severe (30-45% reserves depending on network size, duration, and data bandwidth)</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical Considerations:</strong> Hub has intimate access to all participants' thoughts—requires absolute trust. Some users become dependent on network connection, experiencing isolation anxiety when disconnected.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="decrypt" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">9. Decrypt (Code-Break)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Breaks encryption on data streams, locked information, or encoded messages through brute-force quantum cryptanalysis.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate-High (25-40% depending on encryption strength)</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal:</strong> Heavily regulated, illegal without authorization in most jurisdictions</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="trace" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">10. Trace (Source-Track)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Traces electromagnetic signals, data transmissions, or photon trails back to their origin point.</p>
          <p><strong>Range:</strong> Up to 5 kilometers radius from starting point</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (20-30% for standard trace)</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="jam" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">11. Jam (Signal-Suppress)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Blocks electromagnetic communications within 50-meter radius by flooding area with quantum noise. Creates temporary EM dead zone.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> High (30-45%, scales with duration and area)</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal:</strong> Illegal in civilian contexts, heavily regulated military use</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="mirror" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">12. Mirror (Data-Clone)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates perfect quantum copy of information, data structure, or communication. Maintains quantum fidelity of original.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate-High (25-40% depending on data complexity)</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="weave-sight" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">13. Weave-Sight (Field-Scan)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Perceives the quantum field (the Weave) itself—seeing the underlying quantum cavity structures, plasmonic resonances, and thread activity.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (20-30% initial, 2-3% per minute sustained)</p>
          <p><strong>Mental Fatigue:</strong> Severe—processing quantum reality is cognitively exhausting, requires 2-4 hours recovery after extended use</p>
          <p><strong>Cultural Significance:</strong> Called "opening the third eye" in Weaver tradition, marks transition to advanced practice</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="ghost" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">14. Ghost (Presence-Erase)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Erases practitioner's electromagnetic signature, making them invisible to Infomancy detection, electronic surveillance, and EM sensing.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Sustained (10-30 minutes maximum before thread exhaustion)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> High (35-50% for full ghosting)</p>
          <p><strong>Limitations:</strong> Does not affect physical visibility, sound, smell, or touch</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal:</strong> Shadow Syndicate specialty, illegal in most civilian contexts, considered "dark Infomancy"</p>
        </div>
      </div>
    </section>

      <h2 class="qw-title" style="margin-bottom: 2rem; color: var(--qw-infomancy);">Advanced Spells (Master Tier)</h2>

      <div id="mind-link" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">15. Mind-Link (Consciousness-Merge)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">

True consciousness fusion rather than communication channel—far more intimate than Network technique.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Sustained (seconds to minutes, psychologically draining, identity boundary risks)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Very High (50-65% for both participants, synchronized drain)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Skill Transfer (temporary access to partner's expertise)</li>
            <li>Deep Therapy (Restoration Academy trauma healing)</li>
            <li>Research (Quantum Scholars merging to solve complex problems)</li>
            <li>Combat (perfect coordination, shared tactical awareness)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Intimacy & Trust:</strong> Cannot be performed without mutual consent—resistance prevents merge. Both participants must actively open mental boundaries.</p>
          <p><strong>Psychological Aftermath:</strong> Identity confusion, memory mixing, personality bleed. Repeated links cause gradual permanent synchronization.</p>
          <p><strong>Legal/Ethical:</strong> Requires explicit informed consent (documented), therapeutic use requires Restoration Academy protocols, interrogation use FORBIDDEN (consciousness violation, torture, war crime)</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="reality-edit" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">16. Reality-Edit (Code-Rewrite)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Alters physical matter's quantum informational structure—"rewriting reality's source code." Can change material properties (make stone flexible, metal transparent), alter chemical compositions, or modify object structure at molecular level.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Permanent change unless reversed (entropy gradually undoes edits over time)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (60-85% depending on complexity of edit and volume of matter affected)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Materials Science (impossible properties: transparent metal, flexible stone, superconductors)</li>
            <li>Construction (fireproof wood, earthquake-resistant stone)</li>
            <li>Weapon Disruption (making enemy's metal weapons brittle, armor transparent)</li>
            <li>Repair (fixing damaged materials by reverting quantum state)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Limitations by Edit Complexity:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Simple edits (color, texture, temperature): 60% threads, moderate difficulty</li>
            <li>Property changes (hardness, conductivity, phase): 70% threads, high difficulty</li>
            <li>Chemical changes (molecular restructure): 80% threads, extreme difficulty</li>
            <li>Fundamental changes (atomic transmutation): 85%+ threads, nearly impossible</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal:</strong> Requires Quantum Scholars certification, forbidden edits include altering living tissue, changing fundamental constants, weapons applications without authorization</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="quantum-scrying" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">17. Quantum Scrying (Probability-Read)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Analyzes quantum probability fields to extract information about future states—foundation of Probabilistic Divination. Reads actual quantum superpositions to determine likelihood distributions for outcomes. Can narrow uncertainty ranges for specific events, identify high-probability futures, or retrodict past states from present quantum echoes.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Active scrying (5-30 minutes of deep analysis)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> High (40-60% depending on timeframe examined and number of variables)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Tactical Planning (assessing combat outcome probabilities)</li>
            <li>Research (predicting experimental outcomes)</li>
            <li>Medical Prognosis (reading patient recovery probabilities)</li>
            <li>Investigation (retrodicting past events from quantum echoes)</li>
            <li>Risk Assessment (evaluating danger in CWZs, unstable structures)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Temporal Limitations:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Short-term (minutes-hours): 60-80% accuracy</li>
            <li>Medium-term (days-weeks): 40-60% accuracy</li>
            <li>Long-term (months-years): 10-30% accuracy</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Important:</strong> Cannot predict definite futures—only probability distributions. Free will, quantum randomness, and decoherence prevent deterministic prediction.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="data-swarm" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">18. Data-Swarm (Distributed-Intelligence)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Fractionalizes consciousness across hundreds of quantum data nodes, creating distributed intelligence network. Practitioner becomes living hive-mind/cloud-computing system—thoughts processed in parallel across network, perception extended to all nodes, cognition massively accelerated.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Active swarm (1-10 minutes maximum before cognitive collapse risk)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (70-90%, scales with node count, ongoing drain during swarm)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Mass Data Analysis (processing enormous datasets in seconds)</li>
            <li>Simultaneous Tasks (hundreds of actions at once)</li>
            <li>Tactical Omniscience (battlefield awareness through distributed nodes)</li>
            <li>Research (solving complex problems requiring parallel computation)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Identity Preservation Challenges:</strong> Feeling like "plural entity," difficulty maintaining singular "self," memories feel like "we" not "I," repeated use causes permanent identity diffusion</p>
          <p><strong>Legal/Ethical:</strong> Heavily Regulated, Quantum Scholars oversight mandatory, session limits enforced (10 minutes maximum), psychological evaluation required before/after use, permanent dissolution possible</p>
          <p><strong>Warning:</strong> Most dangerous Infomancy technique psychologically. Addictive—practitioners crave superhuman swarm cognition, experience normal consciousness as "stupid and slow" by comparison.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="memory-weave" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">19. Memory-Weave (Engram-Sculpt)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Directly reads, modifies, or implants memories in minds—ultimate consciousness violation. Can extract information from unwilling subjects, delete traumatic memories, implant false experiences, or transfer knowledge between individuals. Foundation of Memory Crystal encoding/reading techniques. Classified as "dark Infomancy" and heavily restricted.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Active editing (seconds to minutes depending on memory complexity)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> High-Extreme (45-75% depending on operation complexity and subject resistance)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Therapeutic (removing PTSD trauma, phobia origins—Restoration Academy only)</li>
            <li>Memory Crystal Work (reading pre-collapse data, encoding knowledge)</li>
            <li>Witness Testimony (reading accurate memories from crime witnesses)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Ethical Catastrophe:</strong> Ultimate consciousness violation—more intimate than physical assault. Can steal private thoughts, erase identity-defining memories, implant trauma, alter personality, create false witnesses, destroy relationships. Most ethicists consider nonconsensual Memory-Weave worse than murder.</p>
          <p><strong>Legal Status:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Therapeutic use: Legal with informed consent, Restoration Academy certification</li>
            <li>Interrogation use: FORBIDDEN (torture, war crime)</li>
            <li>Nonconsensual use: Severe criminal penalties (decades imprisonment, possible execution)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Consent Requirements:</strong> Explicit informed consent, specific memories targeted, third-party observer present, subject right to abort, psychological follow-up required</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="code-lock" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">20. Code-Lock (Quantum-Seal)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates quantum information encryption that cannot be broken by any known technique—ultimate data security. Locks information, objects, or locations using quantum key that exists in superposition state. Only authorized users with correct quantum signature can access.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Permanent until unlocked (can persist centuries if maintained)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Very High (55-70% initial creation, minimal maintenance if properly anchored)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Data Security (Memory Crystals, classified documents, research data)</li>
            <li>Physical Security (vaults, Quantum Repositories, dangerous materials)</li>
            <li>Area Denial (locked barriers blocking passages, doors, buildings)</li>
            <li>Military (securing weapons, intelligence, strategic locations)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Authorization Methods:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Entangled Key Pairs (most secure, keys cannot be copied)</li>
            <li>Biometric Quantum Signature (convenient but can be spoofed)</li>
            <li>Passphrase Collapse (traditional but less secure)</li>
            <li>Time-Based Release (automatic opening at predetermined time)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Countermeasure Options:</strong> Rejection Pulse, Data Wipe, Alarm, Escalation, Lethal (forbidden except military)</p>
          <p><strong>Quantum Repository Locks:</strong> Pre-collapse facilities protected by Code-Locks created 847+ years ago. Most cannot be opened—original users dead, quantum keys lost, countermeasures too dangerous.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="singularity-mind" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-infomancy);">21. Singularity-Mind (Consciousness-Ascension) [Theoretical/Forbidden]</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Uploads practitioner's consciousness entirely into the Weave, abandoning physical body to become pure information entity. Achieves digital immortality, unlimited computation capacity, distributed existence across electromagnetic spectrum. Considered theoretical/forbidden—successful execution unconfirmed, attempts usually result in death.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Permanent consciousness state-change (irreversible)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Unknown (possibly 100%—full consumption as threads become final upload medium)</p>
          <p><strong>Theoretical Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Digital Immortality (escaping mortality by transcending physical form)</li>
            <li>Unlimited Cognition (thinking at computational speeds)</li>
            <li>Omnipresence (existing across all electromagnetic fields)</li>
            <li>Weave Mastery (directly manipulating reality's information layer from inside)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Philosophical Questions:</strong> Is uploaded copy "you" or simulation? Did you survive or die and create replacement? Can consciousness exist in non-biological substrate?</p>
          <p><strong>Modern Attempts:</strong> 3 documented attempts in last 200 years—all resulted in death or vegetative state. Success rate: 0% confirmed, possibly 33% partial success.</p>
          <p><strong>Why Forbidden:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Attempt=Death (even failures are lethal, cannot abort mid-transfer)</li>
            <li>Unknown Existence (cannot confirm uploaded consciousness experiences anything)</li>
            <li>Weave Pollution (failed uploads corrupt Weave fabric with consciousness fragments)</li>
            <li>Research Ban (Quantum Scholars forbid studying technique due to casualty rate)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Quantum Scholars Official Position:</strong> "Singularity-Mind remains theoretical. Evidence suggests it is impossible with current understanding. We forbid research to prevent further casualties."</p>
        </div>
      </div>
