        <h2 class="qw-title" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">The Science Behind the Magic</h2>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p>
            Pyrosonics manipulates phonons (quantized vibrational energy) and their coupling with plasmonic cavities. Threads create plasmon-phonon hybrid modes, enabling direct control of thermal and acoustic energy at the quantum level.
          </p>
          <p>
            Heat generation works by exciting phonon modes in materials, while sonic weaponry exploits acoustic resonances. The "resonant frequency" of objects is their natural phonon spectrum, which can be amplified to destructive levels through strong plasmon-phonon coupling.
          </p>

    <section id="novice-spells" style="max-width: 900px; margin: 4rem 0;">
      <h2 class="qw-title" style="margin-bottom: 2rem; color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">Foundational Spells (Novice Tier)</h2>

      <div id="ignite" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">1. Ignite (Thermal-Surge)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Generates concentrated heat at target point, raising temperature from ambient to ignition point of flammable materials. Can start fires, cauterize wounds, or provide warmth. Heat appears instantly without flame.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Brief heat spike (1-5 seconds) or sustained low heat (minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Low-Moderate (5-10% for brief ignition, 15-20% for sustained heating)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Fire-starting (campfires, forges, lighting)</li>
            <li>Cooking (instant heat without fuel)</li>
            <li>Cauterization (emergency medical wound sealing)</li>
            <li>Signaling (smoke signals, beacons)</li>
            <li>Warmth (personal heating, cold weather survival)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Safety Warning:</strong> Uncontrolled ignition can cause wildfires, building fires, or burns.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="echo" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">2. Echo (Sonic-Pulse)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Generates directed sonic pulse—concussive sound wave that can knock back targets, shatter fragile objects, or create disorienting noise. Intensity ranges from loud clap to deafening blast.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Instantaneous pulse (sound propagates then dissipates)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Low-Moderate (8-15% depending on intensity)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Combat (knockback enemies, create distance)</li>
            <li>Breaching (breaking windows, doors)</li>
            <li>Crowd control (dispersing groups)</li>
            <li>Signaling (alarm, warning shot)</li>
            <li>Rescue (clearing rubble, alerting rescuers)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Safety Protocol:</strong> Echo can cause permanent hearing damage, concussions, or internal injuries if excessive.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="warm" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">3. Warm (Temperature-Regulate)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Raises or lowers temperature in defined area (3-5 meter radius) by moderate amount (±20°C). Can warm cold spaces, cool hot areas, or maintain comfortable temperature.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (sustained concentration, minutes to hours depending on thread investment)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (12-20% reserves for 1-2 hours of moderate temperature shift)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Climate control (comfort in extreme weather)</li>
            <li>Food preservation (cooling perishables)</li>
            <li>Medical (fever reduction, hypothermia treatment)</li>
            <li>Industrial (process temperature control)</li>
            <li>Camping/survival (warmth without fuel)</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="listen" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">4. Listen (Acoustic-Sense)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Enhances hearing to detect distant sounds, quiet noises, or specific frequencies. Can isolate particular sounds from background noise (conversation in crowd, heartbeat, footsteps).</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (sustained concentration, typically 5-30 minutes before mental fatigue)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Low-Moderate (8-12% reserves for 15-30 minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Surveillance (eavesdropping, reconnaissance)</li>
            <li>Hunting (detecting prey by sound)</li>
            <li>Search and rescue (hearing calls for help, survivors)</li>
            <li>Security (detecting intruders)</li>
            <li>Medical diagnosis (hearing internal body sounds)</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="shatter" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">5. Shatter (Resonance-Break)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Finds and exploits object's resonant frequency to cause structural failure—shattering glass, cracking stone, breaking bonds. Works on brittle materials best.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Brief resonance excitation (2-10 seconds until break)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (10-20% depending on object size and material toughness)</p>
          <p><strong>Material Difficulty:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Easy: Glass, ceramics, crystal (brittle, single resonance)</li>
            <li>Moderate: Stone, concrete, bone (brittle but complex)</li>
            <li>Hard: Wood, plastic (partially ductile, dampens vibrations)</li>
            <li>Very Hard: Metal (ductile, multiple resonances, energy dissipation)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal Status:</strong> Shatter is destructive magic—using it on others' property without permission is vandalism.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="shield" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">6. Shield (Acoustic-Ward)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates barrier that blocks or redirects sound and heat. Can protect against sonic weapons (including other Pyrosonics practitioners), muffle conversations, or insulate from temperature extremes.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Temporary (sustained concentration, typically 10-60 minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (15-20% reserves for 30 minutes of protection)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Combat protection (defending against Pyrosonics attacks)</li>
            <li>Privacy (muffling conversations)</li>
            <li>Insulation (creating comfortable microclimate)</li>
            <li>Sleep protection (blocking noise in loud areas)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Limitations:</strong> Blocks sound/heat but not light or physical matter. Does not replenish air (prolonged use in sealed space causes suffocation).</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="quench" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">7. Quench (Thermal-Drain)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Rapidly extracts heat from target area, dropping temperature and extinguishing fires. Can cool overheated objects, prevent ignition, or create localized cold spot. Opposite of Ignite.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Brief cooling burst (2-5 seconds) or sustained cold (minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Low-Moderate (8-15% depending on heat intensity and duration)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Firefighting (extinguishing blazes)</li>
            <li>First aid (cooling burns, reducing fever)</li>
            <li>Food preservation (rapid cooling, preventing spoilage)</li>
            <li>Industrial (heat treatment, metalworking quench)</li>
            <li>Combat (countering enemy Ignite, preventing traps)</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
    </section>

        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates zone of absolute silence within 20-meter radius. No sound can enter or exit the zone—complete acoustic isolation.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Sustained (5-20 minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate-High (25-40% depending on zone size and duration)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>

            <li>Stealth operations</li>
            <li>Private conversations</li>
            <li>Protecting against sonic weapons</li>

          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate-High (25-45% depending on target size/density)</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical/Legal:</strong> Classified as weapon, heavily regulated, illegal in civilian contexts</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Demolition, breaking locks/barriers</li>
            <li>Combat (rupturing organs)</li>
            <li>Mining, rescue operations (controlled collapse)</li>
          </ul>

      </div>

        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates point of extreme localized heat—up to 2000°C in fingertip-sized area. Can melt metal, weld, cauterize, or cut through materials with precision.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Sustained (seconds to minutes)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (20-35% depending on temperature and duration)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Metalworking, welding, cutting</li>
            <li>Cauterization</li>
            <li>Lock-breaking, precision demolition</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Risks:</strong> Can cause severe burns to caster if focus slips, fire hazard, extreme heat can damage threads</p>
        </div>
      </div>

        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Removes thermal energy from target, lowering temperature rapidly. Can freeze water, cool objects, induce hypothermia, or create ice. Acts as quantum heat pump.</p>
          <p><strong>Duration:</strong> Active cooling (seconds to minutes depending on target mass)</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (20-30%)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Medical (reducing fever/inflammation)</li>
            <li>Food preservation, creating ice</li>
            <li>Fire suppression</li>
            <li>Combat (hypothermia)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Limitations:</strong> Heat must go somewhere (thermodynamics), extreme cold damages threads, requires constant heat source</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <h2 class="qw-title" style="margin-bottom: 2rem; color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">Advanced Spells (Master Tier)</h2>

      <div id="plasma-forge" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">15. Plasma-Forge (Matter-Fourth-State)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Superheats matter into plasma state (ionized gas)—fourth state of matter beyond solid/liquid/gas. Creates contained plasma for cutting, welding, energy projection, or devastating plasma weapons. Can reach temperatures matching stellar cores (15 million Kelvin).</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (70-85%, scales with temperature/volume)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Cutting through anything (plasma torch)</li>
            <li>Devastating ranged weapons</li>
            <li>Materials science (plasma forging)</li>
            <li>Demolition</li>
            <li>Battlefield area denial</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Containment failure catastrophic (uncontrolled plasma explosion), requires constant thread drain, line-of-sight only, extreme heat risks self-immolation, forbidden in civilian contexts.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="resonance-cascade" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">16. Resonance Cascade (Harmonic-Destruction)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Identifies target's resonant frequency then amplifies it to catastrophic levels, causing violent structural disintegration. Works on any solid object—buildings explode, armor shatters, weapons fragment, bones break. "Brown note" on steroids—finding frequency that destroys anything.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> High (50-70% depending on target size/durability)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Demolition (collapsing buildings), anti-armor (shattering defenses), assassination (rupturing organs via bone resonance), siege warfare, structural sabotage.</p>
          <p><strong>Ethical Catastrophe:</strong> Resonance Cascade works on living bodies—can shatter bones, rupture organs, liquify brain tissue by matching biological resonant frequencies. Classified as inhumane weapon, forbidden under war crime conventions.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Requires analysis time (seconds to find resonance), some materials resist (dampened/adaptive structures), practitioner must maintain precise frequency (exhausting), collateral damage extreme, war crime if used on living beings.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="sonic-null" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">17. Sonic Null (Sound-Void)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates zone of absolute silence by generating destructive interference for all sound waves. No sound enters or leaves null-zone—complete acoustic isolation. Can silence explosions, eliminate alarm systems, or create tactical dead zones where communication/coordination impossible.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate-High (35-55%, scales with zone size)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Stealth (silencing footsteps/equipment)</li>
            <li>Tactical (preventing enemy communication)</li>
            <li>Defense (neutralizing sonic weapons)</li>
            <li>Privacy (conversation security)</li>
            <li>Research (acoustic isolation chambers)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Side Effects:</strong> Total silence psychologically disturbing (humans rely on ambient sound), disorientation, tinnitus when leaving zone, communication within zone impossible (lip-reading/hand signals only).</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Only blocks sound (other senses unaffected), zone boundary visible as shimmer, skilled Pyrosonics practitioners can detect/break null with overwhelming sonic force, sustained thread drain.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="thermal-siphon" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">18. Thermal Siphon (Heat-Transfer)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Transfers thermal energy from one location to another instantaneously—freezing one area while superheating another. Can drain heat from enemy weapons (causing malfunction), superheat armor (cooking occupant), or create extreme temperature differentials for tactical advantage.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate (30-50% depending on temperature differential and distance)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Targeted heating/cooling, weapon disruption (freezing firing mechanisms), torture (thermal shock), environmental control, battlefield manipulation (creating fog, ice, steam).</p>
          <p><strong>Combination Power:</strong> Combined with Cryoarchitectonics creates devastating temperature weapons. Cryo practitioner extracts heat, Pyrosonics practitioner weaponizes it elsewhere—perfect synergy.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Requires line of sight to both source and target, distance reduces efficiency, extreme differentials difficult to maintain, conservation of energy applies (cannot create/destroy heat, only move it).</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="infrasound-weapon" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">19. Infrasound Weapon (Subsonic-Devastation)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Generates infrasound (below human hearing, 1-20 Hz)—frequencies that cause nausea, panic, organ damage, loss of bowel control, and at extreme levels, death. Weaponizes sound humans cannot consciously perceive. Called "fear frequency" because it triggers primal terror responses.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Moderate-High (40-60%)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Crowd control (inducing panic/nausea), assassination (untraceable—appears as heart attack/stroke), area denial, psychological warfare, riot suppression.</p>
          <p><strong>Effects by Intensity:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>Low:</strong> Anxiety, unease, sense of presence ("haunted" feeling)</li>
            <li><strong>Medium:</strong> Nausea, dizziness, bowel/bladder control loss, disorientation</li>
            <li><strong>High:</strong> Organ damage, internal bleeding, heart arrhythmia, lung hemorrhage</li>
            <li><strong>Extreme:</strong> Catastrophic organ failure, death (appears natural—no visible wounds)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Ethical Catastrophe:</strong> Infrasound Weapon is invisible, untraceable, and can kill at distance appearing as natural death. Perfect assassination tool. Causes extreme suffering (bowel control loss humiliating, organ damage agonizing). Classified as inhumane weapon worldwide.</p>
          <p><strong>Legal Status:</strong> Forbidden in most jurisdictions—war crime if used in combat, illegal for civilian use, assassination via infrasound classified as premeditated murder with enhancement (cruel and unusual method).</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Affects practitioner if not carefully shielded, low frequencies carry through barriers (hard to contain), difficult to target precisely (area effect), skilled Corpus practitioners can detect/heal damage, leaves subtle evidence (organ damage pattern recognizable to experts).</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="thermodynamic-reversal" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">20. Thermodynamic Reversal (Entropy-Violate)</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Temporarily reverses local thermodynamic time—heat flows from cold to hot, disorder decreases, broken objects self-repair. Violates second law of thermodynamics within limited region for brief duration. Related to Cryoarchitectonics entropy manipulation but using heat as medium.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (75-90%, proportional to reversed entropy)</p>
          <p><strong>Applications:</strong> Instant repair (objects self-assemble), defensive (projectiles de-accelerate), firefighting (flames flow backwards into fuel), tactical (enemy weapons self-destruct in reverse).</p>
          <p><strong>Catastrophic Failure:</strong> Reversed entropy must "snap back" when effect ends—all disorder returns violently plus accumulated interest. Failed Thermodynamic Reversal causes explosive entropy rebound, creating localized reality breakdown or Class 1-2 CWZ.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Brief duration only (seconds), entropy rebound dangerous, extreme thread cost, risks CWZ formation, technically violates natural law (destabilizes reality), requires perfect execution or catastrophic failure.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div id="sonic-black-hole" class="qw-card" style="margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <h3 class="qw-subtitle" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">21. Sonic Black Hole (Acoustic-Event-Horizon) [Theoretical/Forbidden]</h3>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <p><strong>Function:</strong> Creates "black hole" for sound—region where acoustic waves cannot escape, trapped by extreme acoustic impedance gradient. All sound entering event horizon absorbed permanently. Can theoretically trap other wave phenomena (light, heat, quantum fields) if strong enough.</p>
          <p><strong>Thread Cost:</strong> Extreme (85-95%, may require multiple practitioners)</p>
          <p><strong>Theoretical Results:</strong></p>
          <ul>
            <li>Sound cannot escape event horizon (perfect silence within)</li>
            <li>Acoustic energy accumulates inside (pressure builds catastrophically)</li>
            <li>Eventually collapses (explosive release or reality tear)</li>
            <li>May trap other wave phenomena (EM fields, thermal radiation, potentially Weave itself)</li>
          </ul>
          <p><strong>Why Forbidden:</strong> No successful attempts without catastrophic collapse, acoustic event horizons unstable (collapse violently), may create actual black hole (gravity-acoustic coupling), reality fabric tears possible (Weave trapped inside), collapse creates devastating sonic explosion (stored energy released at once).</p>
          <p><strong>Speculation:</strong> Pre-collapse may have weaponized sonic black holes (ultimate siege weapon). Fall possibly related to sonic singularity experiments tearing reality fabric.</p>
          <p><strong>Legal Status:</strong> Research forbidden, theoretical knowledge classified, attempting Sonic Black Hole illegal under reality-threatening technology laws.</p>
          <p><strong>Limitation:</strong> Theoretical only (no confirmed successes), attempts catastrophic, may be impossible (acoustic event horizons require conditions beyond QW system capability), risks reality breakdown.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="qw-card">
        <h2 class="qw-title" style="color: var(--qw-pyrosonics);">Training Requirements</h2>
        <div class="qw-body">
          <ul>
            <li>Understanding of thermodynamics and acoustic physics</li>
            <li>Musical or tonal aptitude (for precise frequency control)</li>
            <li>Emotional control (anger can cause unintended thermal spikes)</li>
            <li>Environmental awareness (to avoid collateral damage)</li>
            <li>Resistance to heat and loud noise (conditioning)</li>
          </ul>
          <h3 class="qw-subtitle">Thread Economy</h3>
          <p>
            Pyrosonics is moderately expensive in thread consumption. While generating heat is relatively efficient, maintaining controlled burns or sustained sonic assaults can rapidly deplete reserves. Harmonic destruction requires intense but brief thread bursts.
          </p>
          <h3 class="qw-subtitle">The Heat Paradox</h3>
          <p>
            Pyrosonics requires emotional heat (passion, intensity, confidence), technical precision (exact frequencies, controlled power), and physical resilience (exposure to heat/sound, sensory overload). This combination attracts passionate, intense personalities while demanding rigorous discipline—a volatile mix.
          </p>
          <h3 class="qw-subtitle">Harmonic Feedback Risk</h3>
          <p>
            Pyrosonics practitioners must be extremely careful with vocal commands. Their spoken phonemes ARE acoustic vibrations—mispronunciation can create destructive interference with their own working, causing immediate catastrophic failure (threads resonating destructively, internal injuries).
          </p>
